# STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

For College Students in India

## GENERAL ONLINE SAFETY
1. Use strong, unique passwords for every account — never reuse passwords.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.
3. Never click on unknown links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
4. Verify the sender's identity before responding to any urgent message.
5. Keep your phone's operating system and apps updated at all times.
6. Never share personal documents (Aadhaar, PAN) with unverified individuals online.

## UPI / FINANCIAL SAFETY
7. Never approve a UPI collect request you did not initiate yourself —
   approving a request sends money, it does not receive it.
8. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, including people
   claiming to be bank officials or customer support executives.

## REPORTING
9. If you are a victim of cybercrime, report immediately at:
   - National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
   - Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7)
